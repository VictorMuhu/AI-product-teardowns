# AI Product Teardowns & PM Playbooks

---

## Why: Speed > Bloat

Modern product management requires moving at the speed of thought.

The old workflow — Figma decks, JIRA epics, alignment meetings — was built for teams who needed consensus before momentum. That era is over. The tools have changed. The leverage has shifted. A single PM with the right prompt architecture can now outpace a ten-person roadmap process.

This repo exists to prove it. Every file here is evidence that the bottleneck is never the idea — it's the drag. Strip the drag, and product thinking moves at the speed of execution.

**Speed > Bloat.** Always.

---

## What: Raw Materials from Public Teardowns

This repo contains the raw prompts, scripts, and architectures used in my public teardowns of enterprise software.

No polished writeups. No thought leadership threads. Just the actual inputs — the directives, the routing logic, the generation patterns — that produced the outputs.

What you'll find here:

- **Prompt architectures** used to interrogate and reverse-engineer enterprise products
- **Agent directives** written for Claude, Replit, Stitch, and other AI-native build environments
- **Workflow scripts** that collapse multi-step PM processes into single orchestrated runs
- **UI generation specs** targeting Zero-UI, B2B enterprise, and executive-grade outputs

These are not templates. They are teardown instruments.

---

## Goal: Open-Source High-Velocity PM Workflows

The goal is to open-source high-velocity PM workflows for builders who want to stop wireframing and start orchestrating.

If you are still spending three days writing a PRD, you are not behind on process — you are behind on tooling. This repo is for the PM who has already decided to close that gap.

Take what is useful. Fork it. Break it. Rebuild it faster.

The only metric that matters is whether you shipped something real.
